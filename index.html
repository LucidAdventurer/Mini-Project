System Information

About PTA Platform

Placement & Training Assessment Platform is a comprehensive system for managing assessments, training materials, and student performance tracking.

Login Instructions

  • Students: Use your institutional email and password to access assessments, training materials, and track your progress.
  • Teachers: Access assessment creation tools, student analytics, and course management features.
  • Guest Users: Try out public assessments without registration. Your results will be temporary.

Key Features

  • Secure authentication with email verification
  • Role-based dashboards (Student/Teacher/Admin)
  • Comprehensive assessment system with multiple question types
  • Training materials library with progress tracking
  • Real-time analytics and performance reports
  • Guest access for public assessments

Need Help?

If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password?" to receive a secure OTP via email. For new account registration, click "Register New Account" and verify your email to get started.

Technical Details

Database: MySQL with comprehensive schema including users, assessments, questions, attempts, and analytics tables.

Security: Password hashing, email verification, login activity tracking, and session management.
