📋 About This Test

This comprehensive aptitude test covers arithmetic, algebra, and geometry topics. It is designed to assess your quantitative reasoning skills and mathematical problem-solving abilities. All questions are multiple-choice with four options each.

💡 The timer will begin immediately when you start

Test Instructions

1
Read Carefully: Read each question and all answer options thoroughly before selecting your answer.
2
Time Management: You have 45 minutes to complete all 30 questions. A timer will be visible at the top of your screen.
3
One Attempt Only: Each question can only be answered once. You can review and change your answers before final submission.
4
Auto-Save: Your progress is automatically saved every 30 seconds. If you lose connection, you can resume from where you left off.

Navigation: Use "Previous" and "Next" buttons to move between questions. You can also use the question palette to jump to any question.

Submission: Click "Submit Test" when you're done. You cannot change answers after submission. Results will be shown immediately.

Scoring: Each correct answer earns you points. There is no negative marking for incorrect answers.
8
No Cheating: Do not use any external resources, calculators, or assistance. This test must be completed independently.
