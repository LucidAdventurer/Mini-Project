🚀 Getting Started as a Teacher

Welcome to PREPAURA's teacher portal. Here's how to get up and running:

  • 1 Log in with your teacher credentials at the login page.
  • 2 Go to Assessments to view, create, or manage tests.
  • 3 Use Manage Groups to organise students into groups for targeted assignments.
  • 4 Upload study materials via Resources for students to access.
  • 5 Monitor student performance through results and analytics on each assessment.

🔧 Troubleshooting
  • If the platform loads slowly, clear your browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete).
  • Use Chrome or Firefox for the best experience.
  • If question editor is unresponsive, try disabling browser extensions.
  • File upload errors may mean the file exceeds the size limit — try compressing it.
  • If students report missing assessments, double-check visibility and date settings.
  • Log out and back in if your session seems stuck or actions aren't saving.
