🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to PREPAURA — your placement training companion. Here's how to get going:

  • 1 Log in or create your student account
  • 2 Browse available assessments from the Assessments page
  • 3 Start a test and track your answers in real time
  • 4 Review your results and score analytics after submission
  • 5 Use Self Assessment to practice with PDFs and level tests

🔧 Troubleshooting
  • ✅ Clear browser cache if pages don't load correctly
  • ✅ Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • ✅ Disable browser extensions if something breaks
  • ✅ Use Chrome or Firefox for best compatibility
  • ✅ Log out and log back in if your session seems stuck
  • ✅ If PDF upload fails, ensure the PDF has selectable text (not scanned images)
